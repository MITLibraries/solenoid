The MIT Faculty Open Access Policy (https://libraries.mit.edu/oapolicy) was established in March 2009 by the faculty to allow open dissemination of their research articles.  The Libraries have been asked by the Faculty Committee on the Library System to assist in implementing this policy using DSpace@MIT.

We would very much appreciate it if you would send us your final submitted manuscript — not the published PDF, which we are normally not allowed to post — of the paper(s) listed below.

You can submit a paper as an attachment by email reply, or use the web form (https://libraries.mit.edu/oasubmit) to upload papers.  We will put the papers in DSpace@MIT, in the Open Access Articles collection (https://dspace.mit.edu/handle/1721.1/49433) with a full citation and link to the published version.
